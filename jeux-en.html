About Video Games

I really like video games where creativity comes into its own. Minecraft is a perfect example because it's so complete. You can build things, create electronic systems, explore, fight and so on, but what I like most is building things. I love Minecraft because it's relaxing and artistic, and I get nostalgic every time I play it because it was the first video game I discovered.

I really enjoy exploration games. The advantage of this type of game is that you can play a little every day without having to invest a lot of time. I don't like competitive games such as Fortinte or Cs Go because you have to train every day to maintain a good level in order to progress. When I've got the time, on public transport or in my spare time, I often play Genshin Impact because the artistic realisation of the game is just breathtaking (graphics, music, lore etc...), especially as it's a game you can play at your own pace.
